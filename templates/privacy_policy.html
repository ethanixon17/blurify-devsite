Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At Blurify, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our video blurring service.

Information We Collect

Video Files: When you upload a video for processing, we temporarily store it in memory only for the duration of processing. Your video files are never permanently stored on our servers and are automatically deleted immediately after processing is complete.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, such as processing times and error rates. This data does not include any personal information or video content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and blur your uploaded videos
  • To provide you with the processed video for download
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All video processing is done in secure, isolated memory environments
  • No video files are permanently stored on our servers
  • All temporary files are immediately deleted after processing
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission

Data Retention

Your video files are processed in memory and deleted immediately after processing is complete. We do not retain any video content on our servers. Processed videos are temporarily cached in memory only until you download them, after which they are permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your video content with any third-party services. All processing is done on our own secure servers using our proprietary blurring technology.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our service without providing any personal information
  • Request information about how your data is processed
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our Support page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
