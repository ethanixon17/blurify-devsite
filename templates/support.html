Frequently Asked Questions

What video formats are supported?

Blurify supports most common video formats including MP4, AVI, MOV, WMV, FLV, and more. The maximum file size is 500MB.

How long does processing take?

Processing time depends on the size and length of your video. Most videos are processed within 1-3 minutes. You can track progress with our real-time progress bar.

Is my video stored on your servers?

No, we never permanently store your videos. They are processed in memory and deleted immediately after processing. Your privacy is our top priority.

What happens if the upload fails?

If your upload fails, please check your internet connection and try again. Make sure your video file is not corrupted and is under the 500MB limit.

Can I blur specific parts of the video?

Currently, Blurify applies a uniform blur effect to the entire video. We're working on selective blurring features for future updates.

Is the service free to use?

Yes, Blurify is completely free to use. We believe privacy protection should be accessible to everyone.

Common Issues & Solutions

Upload not starting

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure the file is a valid video format
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Make sure the file is under 500MB

Processing taking too long

  • Larger files take longer to process
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try uploading during off-peak hours
  • Consider splitting very large videos

Download not working

  • Check your browser's download settings
  • Try using a different browser
  • Ensure you have enough disk space
  • Check if your antivirus is blocking the download
